üé´ Booking & Reservations

How do I book a ticket? ‚ñº
To book a ticket: 1) Search for trips using origin and destination, 2) Select your preferred trip, 3) Choose your seat and class, 4) Enter passenger details, 5) Complete payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket.
Can I book tickets for someone else? ‚ñº
Yes! During checkout, simply enter the passenger's name and contact details. Make sure the information matches their ID for verification purposes.
How far in advance can I book? ‚ñº
You can book tickets up to 120 days in advance. Early booking often gives you better seat selection and may have promotional discounts.

‚ùå Cancellation & Refunds

How do I cancel my booking? ‚ñº
Go to "My Bookings", find your booking, and click "Cancel Booking". Cancellation charges may apply based on how far in advance you cancel.
What is the refund policy? ‚ñº
Refunds depend on when you cancel: 24+ hours before departure: 90% refund, 12-24 hours: 50% refund, Less than 12 hours: No refund. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

üí≥ Payment & Pricing

What payment methods are accepted? ‚ñº
We accept Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, and Digital Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay). All payments are secured with SSL encryption.
How do I apply a promo code? ‚ñº
On the trip details page, enter your promo code in the "Promo Code" field and click "Apply". The discount will be reflected in your total amount. Check our Promo Codes page for active offers.

üë§ Account & Profile

How do I reset my password? ‚ñº
On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a password reset link. You can also change your password from your Profile page when logged in.
Can I modify my profile information? ‚ñº
Yes! Go to your Profile page to update your name, email, phone number, and other details. Changes take effect immediately.
